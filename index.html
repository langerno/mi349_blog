Cooking while in school

By Nolan langer
Published 10/14/2022
Milestone Date Price
Lowest Feb 3, 2015 $1.88
Highest Nov 23, 2018 $8.13
Current Oct 22, 2022 $3.38

I absolutely love cooking but I can never find time to during school, especially with work and homework. I did find an extremely tasty hamburger-helper-esque premade meal that I can mix in some veggies and mushrooms to get a little more flavor and nutrition. It's the Velveeta meals, they are so good. In particular I like to get the Beef Stroganoff flavor, i never really liked beef stroganoff before but this thing to so delicious I could eat the entire package in one night without hesitation if the amount of cheese sauce didn't make me fear for my health. Everytime I make it I struggle to not eat the whole thing, they are extremely good.

I'm realizing that I am very hungry

By Nolan langer
Published 10/14/2022

After my last post I realized that I am, in fact, very hungry and that is being represented through my blog posts. I have left over Velveeta Creamy Beef Stroganoff so as soon as I am done with my homework and back home I am going to eat all of my leftovers. I also have to clean my dishes from when I made it last night, the pan is just sitting out and I'm sure my roommates aren't extremely happy about that but I was very tired after work and working on a project for CSE 335 and completely forgot to clean it before I went to bed. I noticed this morning before work but realized I was already almost late to work and cleaning it would mean being more late to work so I left it. They will be alright I think, I do their dishes more than I'd like anyway.
